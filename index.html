Anne is a five year old Ragdoll who was found in a shelter in Hurlingham, the owner of the shelter is a cat acomulator. Shortly after she was adopted We took her to the vet immediately. The vet told us that she would not make it, and that we should put her down. We decided that we were not going to give up on her and that we would allow her to heal on her own. After one week of being absolutely immobile and still, Anne began to take her first steps. After a little time, she began to walk on her own and began to engage in cat related behaviors. She is the lider of her pack.
